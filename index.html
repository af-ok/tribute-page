Vitalik Buterin Tribute Page

Vitalik Buterin is the young founder of the Ethereum blockchain. He co-created and launched the crytpocurrency at the age of 21.

While Vitalik is quite famous for creating Ethereum his other notable achivements are:

  1. Donating over $1.4 billion to different charitable causes including over $1 billion to Indian COVID relief
  2. Being awarded the Thiel fellowship in order to work on Etheruem
  3. Being awarded a world technology award in 2014 for the Ethereum whitepaper

Vitalik is well respected in the crypto community not just beause of his creation of Ethereum but also because of his repeated push for transparency as well as intellectual exploration inside and out of the crypto space.
